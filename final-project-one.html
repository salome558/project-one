FAQ

Be aware that new Remote Access Requests must be approved by your manager prior contacting the IT Department

  • How long does a remote access takes to receive?
  • It depends of the EMR type, See more about EMR types.
  • Who should provide EMR logins?
  • The practice manages their own EMR accounts.
  • Does HFHT manage all my remote EMR accesses?
  • No, HFHT only manages he HFHT VPN and HFHT tunnel. The rest of the VPNs are managed by the IT provider of each practice.
